Winder is a variable typeface that is loosely inspired by the relativity of body and space to create flow in Feng Shui. Winder’s forms were drawn thinking of the flow of space and how the texture of the typographic form can represent it.

Winder is best used at large sizes and is highly suitable for animation and use on the web. It has a limited Latin character set that is currently in the process of being expanded. Winder interpolates between 2 styles, however more will be added in the future.
